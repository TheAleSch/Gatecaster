# Gatecaster × Your Touchscreen — Make It "Just Work" on Mac

**A free, co-branded macOS driver that turns your touchscreen into a first-class
Mac input device — and a reason for buyers to choose your panel.**

---

## The problem you're selling against

macOS has no built-in driver for USB-HID touchscreens. Out of the box, your panel
acts like a single mouse on a Mac: no real touch, no gestures, no multi-touch. Every
"does it work on Mac?" question in your reviews and support inbox is a lost sale or a
return. Windows has driver support; on Mac, your hardware underperforms through no
fault of its own.

## What Gatecaster does

Gatecaster is a user-space macOS app (no kernel extension, no security prompts
beyond two standard toggles) that turns your panel into a **full Mac input device**:

- **Pointer, tap, drag, right-click** — natural, low-latency.
- **Native multi-touch gestures** — two-finger scroll, pinch-zoom, rotate, swipe —
  rendered as real macOS trackpad gestures, animated and smooth.
- **Momentum scrolling, edge gestures**, palm rejection.
- **On-screen keyboard + virtual trackpad + a Stream-Deck-style control surface**
  (premium features).

The result: your touchscreen feels like an iPad-class surface on a Mac. That is a
**selling point on your product page**, not a support burden.
